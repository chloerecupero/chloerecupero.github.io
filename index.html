MY LIFE

Hi! Welcome to my website! My name is Chloe. I am currently a freshman at the University of Washington studying computer science with a concentration in data science and minoring in French. I'm a Seattle native and have lived here most my life. I spent a few years living abroad in Luxembourg where I travelled a lot and practice French. My family moved back to Seattle after a few years. I am an avid runner and love to be outside in the PNW (hiking, skiing etc.). I work at Oiselle a women's owned running store in University Village and plan to work at Coding for Kids this summer running introduction to programming one week camps! I love Din Tai Fung and also eating Italian food!

I'm super close to my family so its nice to be close at school and still have the opportunitiy to visit them! I have a younger brother Vince who is just 2 years younger than me. My two parents are the best.

One of the things that I touched on earlier that I love most about life is adventure. I love the opportunity to be sponteanous and take risks. You only have one life so I believe you should make the best of it. Some of the ways that I do this is to try to be outside as much as possible. In the summers I try to get out to the mountains once a week to go on a 5-9 mile hike. I love taking advantage of nature and feel especially lucky to be in the PNW. One of my more impressive adventures was during the Summer of 2022, I went to Alaska for a month to backpack on a course through the National Outdoor Leadership School (NOLS). This was the most amazing experiences of my life and solidified my love for the outdoors. I learned leadership skills, risk managemnet and environmental practicum. It was overall a great experience!
